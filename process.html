Process

Simple steps. Better control.

A clear process keeps the work less stressful and makes each decision easier to understand.

How the work moves forward.

The process is designed to make scope, cost, timing and finish expectations visible before and during the job.

Conversation

Discuss the property, goals, access, urgency and any known constraints.

Review

Look closely at the space and decide what needs confirming before work begins.

Plan

Agree scope, materials, sequence and communication points.

Deliver

Carry out the work, check progress and complete the handover carefully.

Handover matters

The final check is part of the build, not an afterthought.

Small details can change how a finished space feels. Chatham Limited treats handover as a practical review: what is complete, what needs explaining, and what the client should know next.
